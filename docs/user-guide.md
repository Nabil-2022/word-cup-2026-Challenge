# User Guide

## World Cup 2026 Prediction Challenge

1. Open the Prediction Challenge.
2. Enter your participant details.
3. Confirm you are 18+ and in an eligible country.
4. Complete every 1/X/2 prediction.
5. Review your entry and accept the rules.
6. Pay the Entry Fee through Stripe Checkout.
7. After successful payment confirmation, your grid is locked.

Entry fee applies. Terms Apply. Eligibility Requirements Apply.

This is a Skill-Based Competition. Winners are determined by prediction accuracy and official rules.
